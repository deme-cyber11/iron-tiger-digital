Privacy Policy

How we handle your information. Written in plain English, not legalese.

The Short Version

We only collect the information you give us (name, email, phone) through our contact forms. We use it to respond to you and provide our services. We don't sell your data. That's it.

What We Collect

When you fill out a contact form or request a consultation, we collect:

  • Name — so we know who we're talking to
  • Email address — so we can respond to you
  • Phone number — if you provide it, so we can call you back
  • Your message — so we understand what you need

Our forms are processed through FormSubmit.co, a third-party form handling service. Your submission is sent directly to our email inbox.

How We Use It

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or consultation request
  • Provide the marketing services you've signed up for
  • Send you project updates and performance reports
  • Follow up on leads or proposals you've requested

We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing emails, newsletters, or promotional campaigns unless you've explicitly opted in.

How We Protect It

Your data is transmitted securely via HTTPS. Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages with Cloudflare DNS, both of which use industry-standard encryption.

Payments are processed through Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor. We never see or store your credit card details.

Who We Share It With

We don't sell, trade, or rent your personal information. Period.

The only third parties that handle your data are:

  • FormSubmit.co — processes contact form submissions
  • Stripe — processes payments securely
  • Google — we use Google Analytics and Google Business Profile tools to understand website traffic (anonymized data only)

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function (like remembering your form selections)
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it

We don't use advertising cookies or tracking pixels from social media platforms.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — ask us what information we have about you
  • Correct — ask us to update inaccurate information
  • Delete — ask us to remove your information from our records
  • Opt out — unsubscribe from any communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at contact@irontigerdigital.com. We'll respond within 2 business days.

Questions About Your Privacy?

Email us at contact@irontigerdigital.com and we'll be happy to answer anything.
