Reputation Services for Contractors

We manage every aspect of your online reputation so homeowners see a business they trust before they ever pick up the phone.

Automated Review Requests

After every completed job, your customers receive a professionally crafted review request via email or text. Our automated system makes it easy for satisfied customers to leave a review on Google, Yelp, or Facebook with just one tap.

Professional Review Responses

Every review your business receives gets a thoughtful, professional response. Positive reviews are acknowledged with gratitude. Negative reviews are addressed promptly and constructively, showing potential customers that you care about getting things right.

Review Monitoring & Alerts

We monitor your reviews across Google, Yelp, Facebook, Angi, and the BBB in real time. The moment a new review appears, you are notified immediately. Negative reviews trigger instant alerts so issues can be addressed before they escalate.

Competitor Reputation Analysis

We track your competitors' review profiles to identify where you stand in your market. This analysis reveals opportunities to differentiate your business and highlights the review volume and rating targets needed to become the top-rated contractor in your area.

Common Questions

Reputation Management FAQs

How do you help me get more reviews?+

We set up automated review request systems that send a text or email to your customers after each job is completed. The timing and messaging are optimized to maximize response rates. Most contractors see a 3–5x increase in monthly reviews within the first 60 days. We also create printed materials and follow-up scripts your team can use on-site.

Can you remove negative Google reviews?+

We cannot remove legitimate reviews, but we can flag and report reviews that violate Google's policies — such as spam, fake reviews, or reviews from non-customers. More importantly, our strategy focuses on generating a consistent flow of positive reviews that push negative ones down and improve your overall rating.

Which review platforms do you manage?+

We primarily focus on Google Business Profile reviews since they have the biggest impact on local search rankings and visibility. We also monitor and manage reviews on Yelp, Facebook, BBB, Angi, and industry-specific platforms relevant to your trade. All reviews across all platforms are tracked in a single dashboard.

How quickly will I see more reviews coming in?+

Most businesses start receiving new reviews within the first week after we launch the automated review request system. The volume depends on how many jobs you complete — the more customers you serve, the more review requests go out. Contractors completing 10+ jobs per week typically see 5–8 new reviews per month.

Do you respond to reviews on my behalf?+

Yes. We craft professional, personalized responses to both positive and negative reviews. Responding to reviews shows potential customers that you value feedback and are actively engaged with your clients. It also signals to Google that your business is active, which can improve your local ranking. We respond within 24 hours of a review being posted.
