Common Questions

Lead Generation FAQs

How many leads can I expect per month?+

Lead volume depends on your trade, service area, and local competition. Most of our home service clients see 10–30 qualified leads per month once campaigns are fully optimized, which typically takes 60–90 days. We set realistic expectations during onboarding based on your specific market.

Are the leads exclusive to my business?+

Yes, 100%. Every lead we generate goes directly and exclusively to you. We never sell shared leads or send the same prospect to multiple contractors. Your investment produces leads that belong only to your business — unlike platforms like HomeAdvisor or Angi that sell the same lead to 3–5 competitors.

What types of home service businesses do you work with?+

We specialize in lead generation for all home service trades including HVAC, plumbing, roofing, landscaping, electrical, painting, concrete, pool services, pest control, and cleaning companies. Our strategies are tailored to the specific search behavior and buying cycle of each trade.

How quickly will I start receiving leads?+

Most clients start seeing their first leads within 2–4 weeks of launch. SEO-driven leads build over 60–90 days as your rankings improve. We focus on quick wins early while building long-term organic traffic that compounds month over month.

What makes your leads different from HomeAdvisor or Angi?+

HomeAdvisor and Angi sell shared leads to multiple contractors, creating bidding wars and driving up costs. We build YOUR online presence so leads come directly to you through your own website, Google Business Profile, and local search rankings. You own the entire system — no middleman, no shared leads, no per-lead fees.
