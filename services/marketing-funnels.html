Common Questions

Marketing Funnel FAQs

What is a marketing funnel for home services?+

A marketing funnel is a step-by-step system that guides potential customers from their first interaction with your business through to booking a job. For home services, this typically starts with a homeowner searching for a service, landing on an optimized page, submitting a quote request, and receiving follow-up communication that builds trust and drives action.

How is a funnel different from a regular website?+

A regular website gives visitors many options and pages to browse. A funnel is laser-focused on one goal: converting that visitor into a lead. Every element — the headline, images, testimonials, and call-to-action — is strategically placed and tested to maximize the percentage of visitors who take action. Funnels typically convert 3–5x better than standard websites.

What happens to leads that do not convert right away?+

Leads that do not book immediately enter automated follow-up sequences. These include professionally written emails and SMS messages that stay top-of-mind with the homeowner. Many leads need 3–7 touchpoints before they commit. Our follow-up sequences recover leads that most competitors lose to slow or non-existent follow-up.

Can you integrate with my existing CRM or scheduling tool?+

Yes. We can integrate your funnel with popular tools like Jobber, ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, and most CRMs. Leads flow directly into your existing workflow so you do not need to change how you manage your business. We handle the technical setup so it works seamlessly from day one.

How do you measure funnel performance?+

We track every step of the funnel: page visits, form submissions, phone calls, email opens, and ultimately booked jobs. Monthly reports break down conversion rates at each stage so you can see exactly where leads are coming from, what is working, and what we are optimizing. No vanity metrics — just numbers that tie to revenue.
