No guessing, no surprises. We follow a clear 4-phase approach that builds your online presence and brings you qualified leads. Here's exactly what happens, step by step.

The Process

Four Phases to Growth

1

Launch & Foundation

Weeks 1-2

You get a professional website that actually brings in leads, plus all the behind-the-scenes tracking to see what's working. We set everything up right so you start seeing results from the beginning.

  • Custom website design and development
  • Google Analytics and Search Console setup
  • Google Business Profile optimization
  • Lead tracking and form integration
  • Initial SEO technical audit and fixes
  • Sitemap, robots.txt, and schema markup
2

Visibility

Weeks 3-6

Now we get you found. We optimize your site so it shows up when homeowners in your area search for what you do, and we make sure your Google Business Profile is working hard to bring in calls.

  • On-page SEO optimization across all pages
  • Local citation building (directories, listings)
  • Review generation strategy and setup
  • Google Business Profile posting schedule
  • Initial blog content creation
  • Internal linking structure optimization
3

Traction

Months 2-3

This is where things start clicking. We look at what's bringing in leads, do more of that, and expand your reach to neighborhoods you're not ranking in yet. You'll see steady growth month over month.

  • Content strategy execution (blog articles, resource pages)
  • Conversion rate optimization (CTA placement, form testing)
  • First monthly performance report delivery
  • Neighborhood/location page development
  • Competitor keyword gap analysis
  • Google Business Profile performance review
4

Growth & Expansion

Months 4+

Your marketing is now a reliable growth engine. We keep improving what works, adjust for busy and slow seasons, and plan ahead so you're always a step ahead of your competitors.

  • Scale what is working based on data
  • Seasonal campaign adjustments
  • New keyword targets and content expansion
  • Quarterly strategy reviews and planning
  • Advanced local SEO tactics
  • Ongoing site maintenance and updates
