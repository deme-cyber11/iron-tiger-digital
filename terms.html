Service Agreement

Simple terms for working together. No legal jargon, no long-term contracts.

The Simple Version

We provide digital marketing services to help your business grow. You can cancel anytime with no penalties, fees, or hard feelings. We're here to earn your business every month.

What You're Paying For

Setup Fee ($300 one-time): Initial website creation, SEO foundation, and Google Business Profile optimization.

Monthly Service ($300/month): Ongoing SEO, weekly content updates, Google Business posts, and monthly performance reports.

Billing is processed securely through Stripe. Your first monthly payment begins after your setup is complete.

Our Commitment to You

  • Deliver the services described in your plan
  • Provide monthly reports showing your progress
  • Respond to your questions within 1-2 business days
  • Be transparent about what's working and what isn't
  • Never lock you into a long-term contract

Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your monthly service at any time. No cancellation fees. No penalties. No questions asked.

Simply email us or let us know you'd like to stop, and we'll cancel your subscription immediately. Your service continues until the end of your current billing period.

Note: The one-time setup fee is non-refundable once work has begun, as it covers time and resources already invested in your project.

Website Ownership

Any website we build for you as part of our service remains your property. If you cancel, you keep your website and all content we created for you.

We'll provide you with all necessary files and access credentials upon request.

Working Together

For best results, we need your help with:

  • Providing photos and information about your business
  • Responding to our questions in a timely manner
  • Following up with leads we generate

The more we work together, the better your results will be.

That's It. Pretty Simple.

We believe in earning your business every month, not locking you in. If you have any questions about these terms, just ask.
